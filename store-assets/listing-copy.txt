FULL DESCRIPTION (4000 char max)
--------------------------------
Barbets turns your friend group into its own private prediction market.

Create a market about anything — will Jake actually run the marathon? Who's the first to leave the party? Will the group hit its fundraising goal? — and bet play-money tokens on how it turns out. No real money ever changes hands.

WHAT MAKES BARBETS DIFFERENT

- Markets can be about someone in your group, and that person can't see it exists — not in their feed, not in their notifications — until it resolves. No favorite-picking, no tipping your hand.
- Bets stay sealed while a market is open. Nobody, not even you, can see who bet what until betting closes.
- Payouts are parimutuel: everyone who bet on the losing side splits their stake among the winners, proportional to how much each person staked. No bookmaker, no house edge.
- Resolution is decided by the group itself — someone proposes what happened, and if nobody challenges it within a set window, it stands. Disagree, and the group settles it by secret ballot.
- Three market types: Yes/No, Over/Under against a line, and Multiple Choice with up to 10 options.
- Seasons let your group reset the economy on a schedule, with standings archived to a Hall of Fame.
- Real push notifications keep you in the loop on markets that need you.

HOW IT WORKS

Create a private group and invite your friends with a code. Everyone starts with the same token balance. Anyone can propose a market; a second member has to endorse it before it opens for betting. Once betting closes, odds become visible as a percentage split, and the group resolves what actually happened together.

No real money, ever. Barbets tokens have no cash value and can never be bought, sold, or withdrawn — it's just a way to make your group chat's arguments and predictions actually mean something.
